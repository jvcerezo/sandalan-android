## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Sandalan is the all-in-one Filipino adulting companion designed for young professionals navigating life in the Philippines.

TRACK YOUR FINANCES
- Log income and expenses with one tap or scan a receipt
- Auto-categorize transactions (500+ Filipino keywords: Jollibee, Grab, palengke...)
- Multiple accounts (BDO, BPI, GCash, Maya, cash)
- Import transactions from GCash, Maya, BDO, BPI, Metrobank CSV
- Monthly budgets with spending alerts
- Bill reminders that never let you miss a due date
- Debt tracker with payoff strategies

SET AND CRUSH GOALS
- Savings goals with progress tracking
- Shared goals for group savings (barkada trip, family fund)
- Investment portfolio tracking
- Split bills with friends — nudge them when they haven't paid

YOUR ADULTING GUIDE
- 6 life stages from "Unang Hakbang" to "Gintong Taon"
- 57 checklist items linked to real app actions
- Step-by-step guides for taxes, contributions, insurance, and more
- Life event triggers that suggest relevant guides when you need them

SMART FEATURES
- AI chat assistant that speaks Taglish
- Spending anomaly alerts ("3x more on food this week")
- Smart home suggestions (bills due, goals close to target)
- Weekly financial recap notifications
- Government contribution calculator (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG)
- 13th month pay calculator
- Retirement projection tool
- Rent vs. buy comparison
- Currency converter with live PHP rates

SECURE & PRIVATE
- Local-first: works offline, syncs when connected
- Document vault for IDs and contracts (encrypted on-device)
- No ads, no trackers, no selling your data
- RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act) compliant
- Data export and account deletion anytime

DESIGNED FOR FILIPINOS
- Categories include Grab, Angkas, Meralco, PLDT, SSS, PhilHealth
- Taglish AI chat ("magkano gastos ko this week?")
- Peso-first with multi-currency support
- Panganay mode for those supporting family

Free to use. No hidden fees. Built with malasakit.
